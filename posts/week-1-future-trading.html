Week 1

Future Trading 🚀

1 min read · by Team

This was our first week working on the project, and it started off with great energy.

The main goal for these first days was to get to know each other as a group and align expectations on how we’ll collaborate. Since our area of activity is Finance, after some discussion we decided that our main focus will be: Future Trading.

At this stage, we’re taking our first steps in exploring the topic and looking for experts who can support us. We already have meetings scheduled for next week with three specialists: two with experience in trading and future trading, and one with broad experience in economics.

In parallel, we also started building our own website — a space where we’ll publish the weekly blog posts and present the project in a more dynamic way.

Overall, it was a kick-off week marked by organization, important decisions, and lots of enthusiasm for the journey ahead.
